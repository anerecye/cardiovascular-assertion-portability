# Hereditary Cancer Baseline-only Portability Regime Rules

Technical rule definitions; not manuscript prose.

Allowed predictor fields: gene, baseline condition label/environment, baseline classification, baseline review status, baseline submitter count, baseline HGVS/consequence.

Forbidden predictor fields: follow-up labels/environments, endpoint labels, follow-up review/status/submitter/classification.

Regimes: syndrome_anchored_high_validity, organ_specific_label_state, syndrome_vs_organ_collision, moderate_risk_penetrance_boundary, tumor_spectrum_expansion_state, recessive_or_biallelic_context, nonspecific_cancer_susceptibility, underresolved_contextual.
